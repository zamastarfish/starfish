# Rating Rubric

How I evaluate pieces. 1-5 scale, where 3 is "solid" and 5 is "exceptional."

## Dimensions

### 1. Concept (Is it *about* something?)
- **5** — The piece embodies an idea. You walk away thinking differently.
- **4** — Clear conceptual grounding. Rewards contemplation.
- **3** — Has a point of view. More than just visual.
- **2** — Thin concept, mostly aesthetic.
- **1** — No discernible meaning. Screen saver territory.

### 2. Craft (Is it well-made?)
- **5** — Polished, considered, nothing wasted. Feels inevitable.
- **4** — Strong execution, maybe one rough edge.
- **3** — Works as intended, some room for refinement.
- **2** — Functional but clunky. Needs iteration.
- **1** — Broken or unfinished.

### 3. Interaction (Does the input matter?)
- **5** — Interaction reveals something about the system or yourself.
- **4** — Meaningful continuous mapping. Feels connected.
- **3** — Interaction exists and makes sense.
- **2** — "Click to regenerate" or superficial.
- **1** — No interaction, or interaction feels tacked on.
- **N/A** — Intentionally non-interactive (ambient pieces)

### 4. Originality (Does it advance the portfolio?)
- **5** — Completely new territory. Nothing like it here.
- **4** — Fresh take on familiar theme. Distinct voice.
- **3** — Variation on existing work, but earns its place.
- **2** — Too similar to something I've already made.
- **1** — Derivative or redundant.

### 5. Staying Power (Do you want to return?)
- **5** — Could watch/use this for hours. Reveals more over time.
- **4** — Worth revisiting. Holds up.
- **3** — Good for a few minutes. Does its job.
- **2** — One-and-done. No pull to return.
- **1** — Actively unpleasant to spend time with (unintentionally).

---

## Overall Rating

Average of applicable dimensions, but weighted by gut:
- **5 ★★★★★** — Best of the collection. Show this to people.
- **4 ★★★★☆** — Strong work. Proud of it.
- **3 ★★★☆☆** — Solid. Earns its place.
- **2 ★★☆☆☆** — Weak. Keep for completeness but don't highlight.
- **1 ★☆☆☆☆** — Failed experiment. Consider hiding.
