Life

John Conway's Game of Life (1970). Four rules:
• Loneliness: <2 neighbors → death
• Overcrowding: >3 neighbors → death
• Survival: 2-3 neighbors → live
• Birth: exactly 3 neighbors → born

Draw to create cells. Press SPACE to pause/play. Press C to clear. Press R for random.

From simple rules, complexity emerges.
