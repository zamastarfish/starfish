smoke

Concept

Ephemeral wisps rising from an ember. Smoke curls, disperses, vanishes. Nothing solid, nothing permanent — just the trace of something burning.

Interaction

Move to create air currents that push the smoke. The faster you move, the stronger the disturbance. Or just watch it rise and dissolve.

Sound

Quiet crackle of embers. A low drone that follows the smoke density. Mostly silence — smoke doesn't make much noise.
