Sublime

Concept

The Romantic sublime — awe in the face of vastness. Caspar David Friedrich's wanderer above the sea of fog. You are small against the infinite. That's not a tragedy; it's a comfort.

Interaction

Move to shift the mist. Click to change vistas. The figure stays small. The landscape stays vast. You remain, watching.

Sound

Wind across distances. Low drone of presence. The sound of being somewhere immense and indifferent. Occasional tones drift in like thoughts.
