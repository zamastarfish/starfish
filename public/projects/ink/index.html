ink

Concept

A drop of ink in water. Watch it bloom, spread, diffuse into chaos. You control the drop; the water takes over from there. About letting go, surrendering control.

Interaction

Click or tap to drop ink. Drag to trail. Choose colors from the palette. Each drop blooms differently — organic, unpredictable, alive.

Sound

Soft plops when drops land. A quiet underwater drone. The diffusion itself is silent — you just watch it happen.
