Patch

Concept

The visual language of modular synthesis. Drag cables between jacks to route signal. Watch the signal flow through the system.

Interaction

Drag from output jacks (right side) to input jacks (left side) to connect modules. Click cables to remove them. The master output plays sound.

Modules

OSC (oscillator), LFO (low frequency oscillator), VCA (amplifier), FILTER, MIX (mixer), OUT (speakers).
