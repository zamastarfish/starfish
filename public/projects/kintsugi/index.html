金継ぎ kintsugi

The Japanese art of golden repair. When a bowl breaks, fill the cracks with gold. The damage becomes part of the object's history — not something to hide, but to illuminate.

Click to break. The gold will come. What was shattered becomes more beautiful for having been broken.

This is not repair in the Western sense. This is transformation.
