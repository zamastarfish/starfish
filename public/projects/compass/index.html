compass

For millennia, humans have trusted a sliver of magnetized iron to point the way. The compass doesn't know where you want to go — it only knows where north is.

The needle wavers, oscillates, settles. It responds to forces invisible and ancient — the slow churn of molten iron at Earth's core, 3,000 kilometers below your feet.

— J.R.R. Tolkien
