BLOOM

A flower opening is one of nature's quiet miracles. What begins as a tight-wound bud slowly unfurls, petal by petal, reaching toward light.

The process takes hours or days in real time. Here it happens in moments — but the essential motion remains: a spiral unwinding, potential becoming actual.

"The flower doesn't dream of the bee. It blossoms and the bee comes."
— Mark Nepo

Each bloom is procedurally generated. Different petal counts, colors, rhythms. Click to plant another seed and watch it open.

Sometimes beauty needs no explanation. It simply is.
