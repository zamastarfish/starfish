THE SPACE BETWEEN STATIONS

Between signals, there is noise. Not silence — noise. The hiss of nothing, the snow of absent pictures, the white roar of entropy.

Tune the dial. Find the ghost stations. Listen to the sound of the universe talking to itself when no one is broadcasting.

Sometimes, almost, you hear something. A voice? A pattern? Or just your pattern-seeking mind, finding shapes in chaos.
