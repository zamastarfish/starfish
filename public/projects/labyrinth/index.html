labyrinth

Like Theseus, you carry a thread. Enter the maze, find the center, then follow your thread back out.

The golden glow marks the center. Your crimson thread marks where you've been. The maze fades behind you, but your thread remains.

"The labyrinth is not meant to trap you. It is meant to help you find the center — and return transformed."
— adapted from the Chartres labyrinth tradition
